My education

St Paul's High School

My favourite class in this school was Biology

NCC selections were held in my school, when I heard this I was bit confused at first but decided to participate in the selections not expecting anything. I told my classmates to join with me and they too agreed for it. My parents to encouraged me to join NCC which was a plus point for me. I did not know much about it, so it was a fresh experience. At the day of selections there were so many young aspiring students of our school who wanted to join NCC; we were tested by some physical and mental activities by our ANO sir and our senior cadets. It was a tough competition, but i was glad that i was selected. I was into some new experience and I was filled enthusiasm. I firstly thought this wasn’t my cup of tea, then later on started enjoying each and every parade.

Karunya University

My favourite course in this university was Cryptography

The beautiful campus, daily morning worship, the hostel prayer cell groups, the amazing staff and students makes a deep, lasting impact on the lives of all the students.I have seen my friend from Karunya University truly “Rise & Shine” all over the world.

Stevens Institute Of Technology

My favourite course in this university is Project Management fundamentals

At Stevens I got to see the Davidson Lab and learn about the history of Stevens.I’ve always had a love for the environment and animals, and because I do not get to see it in my major, I enjoy taking part in events that give me the opportunity to either learn more or help the environment.
